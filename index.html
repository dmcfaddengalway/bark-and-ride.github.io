About the Event

Ride for Those Who Can't See the Road Ahead

Bark & Ride is a charity cycling event held in support of Irish Guide Dogs for the Blind — an organisation that transforms the lives of people with vision impairment and families of children with autism across Ireland.

Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or taking on your first sportive, Bark & Ride offers a route for every rider. Pedal through the breathtaking landscapes of Co. Clare, knowing that every kilometre completed brings independence, confidence and freedom to someone who needs it most.

Clip in. Roll out. Change a life.

Why We Ride

Every Pedal Stroke Makes a Difference

Irish Guide Dogs for the Blind has been transforming lives in Ireland for over 50 years. From guide dogs that give independence to people with vision impairment, to assistance dogs that bring calm and connection to families of children with autism — the impact is life-changing.

All funds raised through Bark & Ride go directly to support the training, placement and aftercare of guide and assistance dogs across Ireland. Your entry fee, your fundraising, your effort — it all adds up to something truly meaningful.
