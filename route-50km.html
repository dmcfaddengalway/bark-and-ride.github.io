Route Overview

The perfect introduction to Bark & Ride. This scenic 50km loop takes in the gentle rolling terrain south of the Burren, passing through quiet country roads, picturesque villages, and lush green farmland. Ideal for first-time sportive riders or anyone looking for a fun, achievable challenge.

Route Highlights

Dromore Wood Nature Reserve

Your first major landmark — a stunning oak woodland and lake system just minutes from the start.

Corofin Village

A charming stop through one of Clare's most beloved heritage villages, rich in traditional music culture.

Inagh Valley Views

The route opens up with panoramic views across the valley floor before looping back toward Barefield.

Elevation Profile

Mostly flat to gently rolling terrain. No major climbs — the largest ascent is a gradual 60m rise over 3km near Corofin. Very suitable for riders of all fitness levels.

Rider Tips

✓

This is a great route if you're new to sportives — the pace is relaxed and the support is excellent.

✓

Start hydrated. Even on a shorter route, Clare's exposed roads can be deceptively windy.

✓

Bring a light rain jacket — weather can change quickly in the Burren region.

✓

Enjoy it! Don't race — soak in the scenery and the atmosphere.
